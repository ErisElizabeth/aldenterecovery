The 5 “I'd Really Rather You Didn'ts” of Al Dente

Gentle, non-judgmental guidelines modeled after the CFSM's original Eight Condiments.

  1. I'd really rather you didn't pretend you are powerless over your own decisions. You are the captain of your own pirate ship now; start steering it.
  2. I'd really rather you didn't make a grueling, shame-filled “moral inventory.” Just clean the expired sauce out of your psychological pantry and move forward.
  3. I'd really rather you didn't force awkward amends on everyone you annoyed while intoxicated, unless you actually owe them money for pizza.
  4. I'd really rather you didn't view a relapse as a catastrophic moral failing that resets your “sobriety clock” to zero. Sometimes the pasta sticks to the bottom of the pot; you just scrape it off and keep cooking.
  5. I'd really rather you didn't build multimillion-dollar rehab empires when the money could be better spent on actual therapy, community support, and lowering the cost of cable.
Al Dente Recovery is a non-commercial, educational, and satirical project. The Flying Spaghetti Monster and Pastafarianism were originally created by Bobby Henderson. This recovery framework is an independent parody and is not affiliated with the official Church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster.
